Who are we?

Artistang Kanal is an emerging art group in Tuguegarao City, that comprises multimedia artists from Cagayan State University - Carig. It was established in 2021.

You will see them, you will hear them, you will feel them,
and you will definitely smell them, for they will stink up every place with intoxicating blend of thoughts and emotions. Despite the discouraging words they've heard
- “Artist ka lang? Walang pera dyan, sa kanal ka pupulutin niyan” they've transformed them into a badge of honor.
They are "kanal" for they are essential conduits towards a better society.

Here's 3/4 of Artistang Kanal and their specialty:
Harvey Gonzales for Traditional art
Jasmin Palattao for Digital art
Friztniyr Nava for Photography

Harvey Gonzales

△ HRV Virus

The works of Pamploneño (Cagayan Valley) Artist, Harvey Gonzales circulates around humanizing the vulnerability of his subject, which is the flawed human condition. He is an observer and a critic. He is fond of tapping into portraying the society, the political injustices, cultural conflicts and other social issues.

Friztniyr Nava

◯ fArt

fArt is a photographer who finds inspiration in the beauty and mystery of nature. His images focus on color, shape, and depth while investigating the relationship between darkness, light, and emotion. His religion is to capture and create stories through lenses.
